How to use the scoreboard

  • Timer: The scoreboard have a programed time for each period:
    • Period 0: 5 mins, This is to allowed time for the player to practice before the game starts.
    • Period 1, 2 & 3: 10 mins, Regular time for a period in ball hockey.
    • Period 4+: 5 mins, Regular time for a overtime period.
    3 buttons are shown on the timer. From left to right, the first button is to set the time. Once clicked the user can modify the time by entering it manually. The play button to start the timer. Finally, the stop button to stop the time.
  • Score: The user can increase or decrease the score of team 1 or 2 by clicking the + or - . The score can not be lower than 0.
  • Period: See above programed time for each period. The user can increase or decrease the score of team 1 or 2 by clicking the + or - . The period can not be lower than 0.
  • Shot: The user can increase or decrease the shot of team 1 or 2 by clicking the + or - . The score can not be lower than 0.
  • Rooster: By clicking the button with an user +, the user can add the number and name of a player. Once it is submited, the player is added in the rooster. The user can only enter one player at a time.
